Return POLICY 

Last updated: November 18, 2023



Thank you for shopping at SnackBar. We are committed to providing you with the finest snacks, and we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If, for any reason, you need to return an item, please review our Return Policy below.


  • 1. Eligibility for returns
  • To be eligible for a return, items must be unused, unopened, and in the same condition as received.
  • Perishable or personalized items are not eligible for return. 
  • Returns must be requested within 30 days from the date of delivery.
  • 2. Return Process
  • To initiate a return, please follow these steps:
  • Contact our customer service team to obtain a Return Authorization (RA) number.
  • Clearly label the package with the RA number provided
  • Include a copy of your original order confirmation inside the package.
  • Ship the item(s) to the address provided by our customer service team.
  • Please note that the customer is responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to an error on our part or a defective product.
  • 3. Refund or Exchange
  • Once we receive your return, our team will inspect the items and process your refund or exchange:
  • Refund: If the return is approved, we will initiate a refund to your original payment method. Please allow 14 days for the refund to be reflected in your account.:
  • Exchange: If you prefer an exchange, we will ship the replacement item(s) once the return is processed.
  • 4. Damaged or Defective Items
  • If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately with photos of the damaged product and packaging. We will promptly address the issue and provide instructions for a replacement or refund.
  • 5. Cancellation Policy
  • Orders may be canceled within 1 hour of purchase. Once an order has been processed and shipped, it cannot be canceled.
  • 6. Contact Us
  • If you have any questions about our Return Policy, please contact our customer service team.



    Thank you for choosing SnackBar. We appreciate your business and are here to ensure your snacking experience is nothing short of delightful!
